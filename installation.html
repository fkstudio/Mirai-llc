INSTALLATION OF SUN ROOF (MOON ROOF)
in autotradingbb

We offer you the possibility of installing Sun Roof to your vehicle. For doing such a service you must buy the vehicle from he auctions which are close to Yokohama. Please see the Auction Locations under Additional Info. If you choose to buy the car from far away auction houses and still you want us to install Sun Roof on your vehicle, you must inform us before buying the vehicle from auction. In that case, you will have to carry out with not only the cost of the Sun Roof itself, but also the transportation cost of the vehicle to our location in Yokohama. For installation of one Sun Roof the price is US$1,200. It takes about 3-4 days to install it properly. Remember, this is After Market installation; it is not original, but we will make it to look as original with automatic opening roof. Please See sample photos.
