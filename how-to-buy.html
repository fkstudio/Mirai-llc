How to buy buy used cars
in MIRAI

Autotradingbb will help you make the purchasing process a simple and enjoyable experience. Our goal is to offer you the best option at the price you want

Our team of professionals will guide you through the entire procedure to find you perfect partner.

Choosing us as your partner will save you money and you will recive excellent customer service and guidance until you find the desired vehicle.

To simply the purchasing process to great extent, we have devised 7 simple steps for you to buy used cars from Japan thought Onteco LTD.

step 1

Fill in the online registration form by entering your details and shipping destination, Let us know if you intend to purchase 1 car for yourself, or if you are a car dealer in your country and are looking for a supplier in Japan.

step 2

LAfter completing Step 1, please check your email for your credentials.
Then, Login to your Account by using your given password that you received in your email.
Your Account will provide you full access to our database of Car Auctions, Shipping Schedule and other benefits.
You can also check what vehicles and machinery is being traded on all Japanese Car Auctions.
After logging in, you can further proceed to Step 3 for purchasing.

step 3

Although you have access through our website to Japanese auctions, In order to start bidding and purchasing cars, you need to makeg a REFUNDABLE DEPOSIT.
The deposit amount is 1000 USD. We don't keep this money, it will reduce from the value of the car after your purchase or it can be fully refunded in case you no longer require a purchase of car.

step 4

You can start placing bids on any car that is being sold in Japan, once you become familiar with the Japanese Used Car market.

You will have the selection to choose from about 65,000 active auction lots being traded at any given time. If you want to learn about market prices and car specifications, you can consult our Sales Rep at any time. To place a bid, just simply find the auction lot which interests you and enter the maximum bid in C&F USD (the maximum price for which you are willing to buy the given car for).

step 5

As soon as we receive your bid(s), we will try to buy the car from the auction at the lowest price possible ( up to your maximum bid).
A successful maximum bid means that we managed to purchase the car for you and will inform you immediately after the auction ends.
The following day after the successful purchase, we will send an invoice for buying the car from the auction.
The full invoice amount must be transferred within 3 working days after the auction date. This is a Strict requirement of all auction houses in Japan.
We cannot receive your car until we forward your money to the auction house for the full amount of the car cost.
Failure to make full payment within 3 working days will incur steep penalties from the auction house, which we must apply to your PURCHASE ACCOUNT and deduct the charges from your deposit.

step 6

The car that you have bought can be shipped as it is or follow After-Selling Service.
Our company can ship cars as they are. In this case the procedure is straight-forward: cars are delivered by trailer from the auction site to our yards in Yokohama and Osaka.
In case you require additional services, our company can provide tires exchange and fitting, as well as full car dismantling to spare parts.
Please contact us for more information and other services for your car.

step 7

The cars that you bought will be shipped accordingly.
As soon as the cars are ready to ship to your country, we must finalize all relevant documentation and procedures for export.
Our Logistics Team will coordinate with you personally the entire process to the fastest routes to your destination.
In case if you require a more detailed explanation, please do contact us at any time.
